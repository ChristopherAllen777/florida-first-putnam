>About Adam Putnam

Adam Putnam wants every Floridian to have the freedom and opportunity to pursue his of her own American Dream. He has a clear vision for the future of Florida. It's a Florida in which we can all prosper and grow. Adam is someone who gets the job done and will always stand up for his conservative principles and our Florida values. Adam's desire has always been to serve his community, friends, neighbors, state, and country.




>Florida Grown Purpose and Mission

This mission of Florida Grown PC, a statewide political committee, is to support and discuss candidates for statewide, multi-county, legislative, county or municipal office in Florida.
